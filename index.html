IIST OPEN TEST FOR ASPIRANTS of JEE Main

ALL INDIA OPEN MOCK TEST FOR JEE MAIN

iOTA is an initiative by students of Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST ) , Thiruvananthapuram to provide quality paper solving practice for students preparing for JEE Main. iOTA is held in 2nd week of January. The question paper is designed by top rankers at IIST with guidance by their mentors and with great effort and research on the trends and their personal experience of giving JEE . This year the examination is conducted on specially designed software to give the students a real feel of JEE exam.

Our aim is to reach every JEE aspirant who is not getting quality practice due to lack of financial support and lack of right guidance .After registering through the Google form the candidate will get a username and password with which he can login in the website

Candidates will be given access to a website and also an app. Students who do not have laptops or desktops can just install the app in their phone and give the test on it
